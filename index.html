Background

Welcome to Shxrk Surf Co, where passion meets fashion in our unique clothing designs. As a small business based in the sunny state of California, we've been crafting apparel since our establishment in 2023. At Shxrk Surf Co, we believe in giving back to the environment that inspires us. That's why 5% of all sales contribute to @theoceancleanup, supporting efforts to keep our oceans nice and clean! Join us on this journey of style and sustainability, making waves in the world of fashion with purpose.
